Simple Rental Requirements

Renting a car or truck from us is straightforward. Here are the basic requirements:

Valid Driver's License

A valid Nigerian driver's license held for at least 1 year. International Driving Permits are also accepted for visitors.

Minimum Age

Renters must be at least 23 years old. Some vehicle categories may have higher age requirements.

Payment Method

A valid credit or debit card in the renter's name for payment and security deposit.

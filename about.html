About Car Truck Rental

Your trusted partner for hassle-free vehicle rentals in Nigeria.

Our Story

Founded in *2010*, Car Truck Rental began with a vision to revolutionize vehicle rental services across Nigeria. We recognized the growing need for reliable, affordable, and flexible transportation solutions for individuals and businesses alike. Starting with a modest fleet, our focus has always been on customer satisfaction and operational excellence.

Over the past *15 years*, we have steadily grown into a leading rental service, expanding our fleet to include a diverse range of modern cars and powerful trucks. Our journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of quality service, technological adoption, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs.

From the bustling streets of Lagos to the industrial hubs of Port Harcourt and the administrative capital of Abuja, we are proud to serve communities nationwide, making every journey smoother and more convenient.
